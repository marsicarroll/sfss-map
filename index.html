This map depicts the contribution and distribution networks associated with each issue of The Song Fishermen's Song Sheet (1928). Select an issue to explore its participants and circulation pathways.

Blue lines represent contributions sent to editor Andrew Merkel in Halifax. Red lines represent distributions sent outward after printing.
